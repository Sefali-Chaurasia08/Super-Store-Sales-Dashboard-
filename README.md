📊 Sales Superstore Analysis (Revenue & Profitability)
🔎 Project Overview

This project analyzes the Sales Superstore dataset using Power BI to evaluate both revenue performance and profitability trends.
Two dashboards were created from the same dataset.

📈 Revenue Analysis – Key Highlights
Total Revenue: 11.49M
Total Cost: 11.20M
Total Profit: 286.40K
Profit Margin: 2.49%

🔍 Insights:
High sales but very low profit margin (2.49%).
Revenue peaks in Q4 (Sept–Dec), indicating seasonal growth.
Standard shipping mode generates the highest sales.
Revenue is concentrated in a few major cities.

💰 Profitability Analysis – Key Highlights
Technology category generates highest profit.
Furniture category contributes low profit despite good sales.
High discounts negatively impact margins.
Identified top loss-making products affecting overall profitability.

🛠 Tools & Skills Used
Power BI (Dashboard Development & DAX)
Data Cleaning & Business Insight Generation

🎯 Business Impact
This project demonstrates that high revenue does not guarantee high profitability.
Cost optimization, discount control, and category-level strategy are essential for improving overall margins.
